Bhedaghat Marble Rocks

Jabalpur

Magnificent marble cliffs along the Narmada River that rise up to 100 feet, creating a spectacular gorge. Take a boat ride through this natural wonder to witness its changing colors.

4.6 (692 reviews)

Bhedaghat is the only place in Narmada river's course of about 1312kms where the river falls into a 30-meter deep gorge and flows between marble rocks. These marble rocks appear in different colors as sunlight reflects at different angles. The most magical time to visit is during full moon nights when the marbles glow in the moonlight. The boat ride costs approximately ₹100 per person and lasts for 30-40 minutes.

Best Time to Visit:

October to March is ideal when the weather is pleasant.

Timings:

Boat rides available from 6 AM to 8:30 PM

Dhuandhar Falls

Jabalpur

A spectacular waterfall where the Narmada River narrows down and plunges into a gorge, creating a misty atmosphere that gives it the name "Dhuandhar" meaning "smoke cascade".

4.5 (763 reviews)

Dhuandhar Falls is located about 25 km from Jabalpur railway station. The waterfall drops from a height of 10 meters, creating a cloudy appearance as water particles rise up like smoke. There's a cable car ride available that offers a panoramic view of the falls and the surrounding area. The area around the falls has been developed with viewing platforms, gardens, and souvenir shops.

Best Time to Visit:

Monsoon season (July to September) when the waterfall is at its fullest.

Entry Fee:

The viewing area charges a nominal fee. Cable car rides are priced separately.

Jhoteshwar Temple

Narsinghpur

A sacred and gorgeous temple dedicated to Raj-Rajeshwari Tripur Sundari goddess, offering spiritual solace amidst natural surroundings.

4.5 (982 reviews)

Jhoteshwar Temple is located in Shyamnagar, Madhya Pradesh. The temple complex includes the main Tripur Sundari temple, Lodheshwar temple, and Hanuman tekari. Visitors can also find natural rock formations and a Shivling. The temple is situated in a serene environment, making it perfect for meditation and spiritual contemplation.

Best Time to Visit:

Year-round, but especially during Hindu festivals.

Timings:

Opens early morning and closes at 9 PM

Osho Ashram

Narsinghpur

A place of historical significance where Osho used to meditate from the age of 14, offering a serene environment for meditation and self-discovery.

4.2 (11 reviews)

Osho Ashram is located in Gadarwara town on the banks of the Shakkar River in Narsinghpur district. This is where Osho (Bhagwan Shree Rajneesh) used to come and meditate from his early years. The ashram is surrounded by lush greenery, creating an ideal atmosphere for meditation. Visitors can participate in meditation sessions and learn about Osho's teachings.

Best Time to Visit:

Throughout the year, especially during meditation retreats.

Activities:

Meditation, yoga, and spiritual discussions

Chausath Yogini Temple

Jabalpur

An ancient temple dedicated to the 64 Yoginis, built on a hilltop near Bhedaghat offering panoramic views of the Narmada River and surrounding landscapes.

4.3 (157 reviews)

Chausath Yogini Temple is one of the oldest heritage sites in Jabalpur, dating back to the 10th century. The temple is circular in shape and contains 64 small chambers, each dedicated to a different Yogini. Built on a rocky hilltop, the temple requires climbing about 150 steps. The architectural style is unique and the temple offers a spectacular view of the Narmada River and marble cliffs.

Best Time to Visit:

Early morning or late afternoon to avoid the heat and catch beautiful views.

Historical Significance:

One of only four Yogini temples preserved in India today.

Madan Mahal Fort

Jabalpur

A historical fort perched on a hilltop offering panoramic views of Jabalpur city. Built by the Gond ruler Raja Madan Shah in the 11th century.

4.1 (420 reviews)

Madan Mahal Fort is situated on a rocky hill at an elevation of about 500 meters. While much of the fort is in ruins now, its strategic location and historical significance make it a must-visit. Near the fort is the famous Balancing Rock, a geological wonder where a huge boulder is balanced naturally on another rock. The fort provides a breathtaking view of the entire city and surrounding landscapes.

Best Time to Visit:

Early morning or evening for better views and comfortable weather.

Entry Fee:

Free of charge

Travel Tips

Essential information to help you plan your perfect trip to Narsinghpur and Jabalpur regions.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Winter season offers pleasant weather with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C, making it the best time for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

July to September

Monsoon season is ideal for seeing the waterfalls in their full glory, but some activities might be restricted due to heavy rainfall.

April to June

Summer can be hot with temperatures reaching up to 45°C. Early morning and evening visits are recommended during this season.

How to Reach

By Air

The nearest airport is Jabalpur Airport (JLR), which is well-connected to major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Hyderabad. From the airport, you can hire a taxi to reach your destination.

By Train

Jabalpur Junction is a major railway station with good connectivity to all parts of India. Narsinghpur also has a railway station with limited train services.

By Road

Both Jabalpur and Narsinghpur are well-connected by roads. Regular buses operate from nearby cities, and the roads are in good condition for private vehicles.

Local Transportation

Taxis and Auto Rickshaws

Available throughout both cities. Auto-rickshaws are economical for short distances, while taxis can be hired for full-day sightseeing.

Local Buses

Public buses connect various parts of the cities and surrounding areas. They are budget-friendly but might be crowded during peak hours.

Rental Services

Car rental services are available in Jabalpur, which is convenient for exploring multiple destinations at your own pace.

What to Pack

Clothing

Light cotton clothes for summer, warm clothing for winter, and a light jacket for evenings. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for exploring.

Photography

Camera with extra batteries and memory cards. The natural landscapes and architectural marvels offer excellent photo opportunities.

Essentials

Sunscreen, insect repellent, personal medication, and a basic first-aid kit. Carrying a water bottle and some snacks is recommended for day trips.
